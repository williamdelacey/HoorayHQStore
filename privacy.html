Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Hooray HQ ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy and handles your personal information in accordance with the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it.

1. What we collect

  • Order & enquiry details: your name, email, phone, location/suburb, event date, budget range, and any notes you provide.
  • Payment information: card payments are processed securely by Stripe. We do not store your full card details.
  • Basic technical data: standard website logs (e.g. via our hosting provider, Cloudflare).

2. How we use it

  • To respond to enquiries, prepare quotes, and fulfil your orders and bookings.
  • To communicate with you about your order or event.
  • To improve our products and service.

3. Who we share it with

We use trusted third-party services to run our business, including Stripe (payments), Zoho CRM (managing enquiries and customer records), and Cloudflare (website hosting). We share only what's necessary for these services to function. We do not sell your information, and we won't share it with others except as required by law.

4. Photos of our work

We love sharing photos of our installations on our website and social media. Where a photo identifies you or your child, we'll seek your consent first.

5. Your rights

You can ask to see the personal information we hold about you, request corrections, or ask us to delete it (where we're not required to keep it). Email hello@hoorayhq.co.nz and we'll help.
