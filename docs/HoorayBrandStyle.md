## Brand Story\
\
### Who We Are\
Hooray HQ is a party hire brand based in Whangaparaoa, Auckland, New Zealand. We help people mark their moments with ease and a bit of wow.\
\
### What We Do\
We offer balloon garlands, neon sign hire, and photo booth services that bring the fun. Whether it's a backyard birthday, a corporate do, or anything in between, we sort the statement pieces so you can focus on the good times.\
\
### Our Mission\
"Parties made easy, memories made bright."\
\
We're here to make celebrating in Whangaparaoa and across Auckland simple and stylish, with ready-to-go party hire that looks great and just works.\
\
### What Drives Us\
We love seeing a space transform and a crowd light up. If it helps you celebrate better \'97 brighter, bolder, more you \'97 that's our sweet spot.\
\

## Logo Usage\
\
### Primary Logo\
The primary Hooray HQ logo features "HOORAY" in a playful arched coral brush script with "HQ" in white inside a coral circle positioned to the lower right, accompanied by decorative confetti, dots, and zig-zag elements.\
\
**When to use:**\
- Marketing materials\
- Social media headers\
- Print collateral\
- Large format applications\
- Anywhere the playful arch can be clearly seen\
\
### Secondary Logo (Straight Lockup)\
The secondary logo features "HOORAY" in straight/flat coral brush script with "HQ" in the coral circle beside it.\
\
**When to use:**\
- Digital platforms where the arched logo loses legibility at small sizes\
- Email signatures\
- Social media profile images\
- App icons\
- Website headers\
- Constrained horizontal spaces\
\
### Logo Variations\
- **On light backgrounds:** Coral logo with white HQ\
- **On dark backgrounds:** White logo with coral HQ circle\
- **On photos:** Use version with best contrast\
\
### Clear Space\
Maintain clear space around the logo equal to the height of the "HQ" circle on all sides.\
\
### Minimum Size\
- Print: 25mm width minimum\
- Digital: 100px width minimum\
\
### Logo Don'ts\
- \uc0\u10060  Don't stretch or distort\
- \uc0\u10060  Don't rotate (except the intentional arch)\
- \uc0\u10060  Don't change colors\
- \uc0\u10060  Don't add effects (shadows, outlines, gradients)\
- \uc0\u10060  Don't separate the elements\
- \uc0\u10060  Don't place on busy backgrounds without sufficient contrast\

## Typography\
\
### Primary Typeface (Display)\
**Brush Script / Handwritten Style**\
\
Used for:\
- "HOORAY" wordmark\
- Headlines\
- Hero text\
- Emphasis moments\
- Celebratory messaging\
\
Characteristics: Playful, energetic, human, confident\
\
### Secondary Typeface (Body)\
**Clean Bold Sans-Serif**\
\
Used for:\
- "HQ" in logo\
- Body copy\
- Captions\
- Subheadings\
- Supporting information\
- Functional text\
\
Characteristics: Clear, modern, readable, grounded\
\
### Typography Hierarchy\
- **H1 (Hero):** Primary brush script, large, coral\
- **H2 (Section Headers):** Secondary sans-serif, bold, black or coral\
- **H3 (Subheadings):** Secondary sans-serif, medium weight, black\
- **Body:** Secondary sans-serif, regular weight, black\
- **Captions:** Secondary sans-serif, small, black or grey\
\

## Graphic Elements\
\
### Confetti\
Playful scattered shapes in coral, pink, and accent colors. Use to add energy and celebration to designs.\
\
**Usage:**\
- Scatter around key elements\
- Frame important content\
- Add movement to static designs\
- Don't overdo it \'97 less is more\
\
### Doodle Dots\
Small circular dots in brand colors. Simple, fun, versatile.\
\
**Usage:**\
- Bullet points\
- Decorative accents\
- Pattern fills\
- Connecting elements\
\
### Zig-Zag Lines\
Hand-drawn style zig-zag lines in coral or pink.\
\
**Usage:**\
- Underlines for emphasis\
- Dividers between sections\
- Playful borders\
- Dynamic accents\
\
### Usage Guidelines\
- Use graphic elements to enhance, not overwhelm\
- Maintain the playful, hand-crafted feel\
- Keep it balanced \'97 pair busy elements with clean space\
- These elements are part of the brand personality \'97 use them consistently\

## Tone of Voice\
\
### Voice Attributes\
\
**Confident**\
We know what we're doing. We're the party hire experts you can trust.\
\
**Punchy**\
Short, snappy, memorable. Get to the point with energy.\
\
**Warm**\
Approachable and human. We're here to help you celebrate.\
\
**Celebratory**\
Upbeat and positive. Every moment is worth marking.\
\
### Writing Style\
\
**Do:**\
- Keep it conversational and friendly\
- Use short sentences and paragraphs\
- Be direct and clear\
- Show enthusiasm without being over-the-top\
- Use "we" and "you" to create connection\
\
**Don't:**\
- Use corporate jargon or stuffy language\
- Overcomplicate things\
- Be passive or uncertain\
- Use excessive exclamation marks!!!\
- Take yourself too seriously\
\
### Key Phrases\
- "Parties made easy, memories made bright"\
- "Ready-to-go party hire that just works"\
- "Mark your moments"\
- "Bring the wow"\
- "Celebrate better"\
\
### Example Copy\
\
**Good:**\
"Need a photo booth for Saturday? We've got you. Book online and we'll deliver the fun."\
\
**Bad:**\
"Hooray HQ provides comprehensive photographic booth rental solutions for your upcoming celebratory event requirements."\

## Favicon\
\
### Design\
The Hooray HQ favicon uses the "H" from the HOORAY wordmark in coral brush script inside a coral circle, or the simplified "HQ" coral circle from the logo.\
\
### Specifications\
- **Format:** PNG with transparent background, ICO for browser compatibility\
- **Sizes:** 16x16px, 32x32px, 64x64px, 128x128px\
- **Design:** Coral circle with white "H" or "HQ"\
- **Usage:** Browser tabs, bookmarks, app icons, shortcuts\
\
### Export Guidelines\
- Export at multiple sizes for different platforms\
- Maintain the bold, simple design for recognition at tiny sizes\
- Use transparent background for flexibility\
