# How to Build Your Hooray HQ Balloon Garland

*You've got this! Follow these steps and you'll have a stunning garland up in about an hour.*

---

## What's in Your Kit

- ✅ Balloons in your chosen colour palette (3 sizes: small, medium, large)
- ✅ Balloon garland tape (holey strip)
- ✅ Balloon tie tool
- ✅ Command hook strips

**You'll also need:**
- A balloon pump (hand or electric — not included). An electric pump makes this so much faster!
- Scissors

---

## Step 1 — Inflate Your Balloons

Inflate balloons in batches by size:
- **Small (5")**: softball size
- **Medium (11")**: grapefruit size  
- **Large (16")**: volleyball size

**Tips:**
- Don't over-inflate. Slightly under-inflated balloons are easier to attach and look better.
- Use the tie tool: thread the balloon neck through the notch, spin the tool — it ties itself!
- Keep a rough ratio of: 40% medium, 35% small, 25% large.

---

## Step 2 — Thread Onto Garland Tape

The garland tape has holes every few centimetres.

- Poke the knotted end of the balloon through a hole. It will stay put.
- Alternate sizes and colours as you go — no pattern needed, organic looks best!
- Fill every hole on both sides of the tape.
- Leave about 10cm free at each end (for hanging).

---

## Step 3 — Fill the Gaps

Once all balloons are on, scrunch the garland together slightly so the balloons cluster and fill any gaps. Smaller balloons can be tucked in between larger ones without tape — just wedge them in.

Stand back and check from a distance. Rotate the garland to find the fullest side for display.

---

## Step 4 — Hang It Up

**Option A — Command Strips (walls, no holes):**
1. Clean the wall with a dry cloth.
2. Press strip firmly for 30 seconds.
3. Wait 1 hour before hanging.
4. Loop the end of the garland tape over the hook.

**Option B — Nails or hooks** (most secure for longer garlands):
1. Hammer small nails or push-pin hooks at each end.
2. Loop garland tape over and adjust position.

**For a swooping arch shape:** Hang higher in the middle and lower at the ends, or hang both ends at the same height and let it droop naturally.

---

## Tips for a Beautiful Result

- **Cluster different sizes together** — it creates that lush, full look.
- **Don't stress about perfection** — slight imperfections make it look handmade and beautiful.
- **For a wall:** 2–3 attachment points are enough for a 4m garland.
- **For a doorway arch:** Attach tape to the top of each door frame and curve it overhead.
