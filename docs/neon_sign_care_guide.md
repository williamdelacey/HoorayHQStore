# Hooray HQ — Neon Sign Care & Use Guide

*Thanks so much for hiring from Hooray HQ! Here's everything you need to know to keep your sign glowing safely.*

---

## Setting Up Your Sign

1. **Find your spot.** Choose a flat, stable surface or hang the sign from its pre-attached hanging points. The sign comes with 2 hanging holes — use the provided screws, nails, or Command hooks.

2. **Check the power cable.** Gently uncoil the cable — don't bend sharply or pull at the plug.

3. **Plug in and switch on.** Use the in-line switch on the power cable. The sign should light up immediately. If it doesn't, check the plug is fully seated.

4. **Best results.** Signs look best in lower lighting or at night. For daytime, dim the room or position near a wall away from direct sunlight.

---

## Safe Use

- **Indoor use only.** These signs are not weatherproof. Keep away from rain, direct sunlight, and humidity (e.g. outdoors, bathrooms, or near pools).
- **Keep away from water.** No liquids near the sign.
- **Don't leave unattended for long periods.** Switch off if leaving the room for extended periods.
- **Children and pets.** Keep out of reach — the power cable and backing are not toys.
- **Don't modify.** No cutting, bending, or adjusting the sign structure.

---

## Hanging Tips

- **Command strips** (Large, rated to 2–3kg): Clean the wall surface, press strip firmly, leave 1 hour before hanging.
- **Adhesive hooks**: Same approach — clean wall, press, wait.
- **On a shelf or table**: The sign can stand upright leaning against a wall — no hanging needed.
- **On a backdrop or stand**: Use cable ties through the hanging holes.

---

## Packing for Return

Please pack the sign the way it arrived:

1. Switch off and unplug.
2. Allow to cool for 5 minutes before packing.
3. Coil the power cable loosely around itself — **do not wrap tightly**.
4. Place the sign face-up in the box/case, with foam or padding underneath.
5. Secure the lid. Don't stack anything on top.

---

## Return

Return the sign by the agreed time to avoid late fees. If you need extra time, contact us before the return time and we'll do our best to extend (subject to availability).

Return condition check: signs should be free of marks, scratches, or damage. Any issues will be documented against the bond.
