Hi, I'm Hannah!

For years, I've had the privilege of working with youth centres and churches, creating fun and inviting environments for kids and young people. Whether it's through a themed event or a decorated space I've always loved finding ways to bring excitement and energy to a room.

Since having my own kids, I've loved creating special celebrations for them and their friends. From themed backdrops to oversized balloon garlands, I'm constantly dreaming up ways to bring their ideas to life, creating party spaces that make them walk in and think, "Wow, this is the best day ever!"

That love for celebrating life's special moments inspired me to start Hooray HQ. My hope is that through this business, I can help make your birthday, anniversary, graduation, baby shower, or special event feel just that little bit more elevated. Because I know the moments that matter most deserve a HOORAY!
